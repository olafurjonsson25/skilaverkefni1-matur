About the restaurant

About Us

Welcome to the Hungry Llama, a family-owned restaurant that offers a unique and unforgettable dining experience. Our restaurant is inspired by the bold flavors and warm hospitality of South America, and we pride ourselves on serving fresh and locally sourced ingredients. At the Hungry Llama, we believe that food is not just a necessity, but an adventure, and we aim to take our customers on a culinary journey through our carefully crafted menu. Whether you're in the mood for a hearty meal or a light bite, our diverse menu has something for everyone. We also offer a wide selection of refreshing beverages and cocktails to complement your meal. Come join us at The Hungry Llama, where good food meets great company.

Information & training

We want all new employees to receive all necessary information in the right time and form when appropriate. We want changes and other information to be communicated to all employees in a clear and secure manner. We want all employees to receive proper and timely training on the job. We want all employees to know our training ladder and have full access to information about courses and professional development

Communication & fairness

We want all communication in the workplace to be responsible and professional. Joy isn't forbidden, but backtalk is. We want all employees at The Happy Llama to be role models for new employees. We want The Happy Llama's customers to experience interacting with The Happy Llama's staff as professional, cheerful and solution-oriented. We ensure that everyone has equal rights to professional development and wages, we never discriminate based on gender, sex, religion or nationality. We want the door to flexibility to open in both directions. We can't demand flexibility if we can't provide it in return.
